Welcome to Fit For Fitness

Fit for Fitness is a chain of gyms originating from Springfield, Missouri. Our philosophy is that you're perfect the way you are, but we're here if you want to get healthier. We have many personal trainers to help you achieve this goal. We also have many machines and weights and other exercise tools to if you'd rather work out yourself. We've worked hard to make a judgement-free workout enviornment for the enjoyment of everyone.

Workout Machines

We have a wide variety workout machines at your disposal! You're free to come in and use any of them whenever you have the time.

Personal Trainers

If you're new to working out, or just want some help, then you're in luck! We have many personal trainers who would be more than happy to help you!

Nutrition Information

If you're looking to better your diet then we can help you with that too! We have many courses and person diet coaches to help you start eating better.
